Example:

1. How can you be environmentally friendly at work?
In a tech company, you can be environmentally friendly by using less paper, turning off computers when not in use, and recycling plastic, paper, and electronics. People should bring their own water bottles and coffee cups. Workers who stay at home don’t have to travel, so they help the environment too.

2. What kind of incentives could a green company offer to its employees?
A green company could give rewards like gift cards, free lunches, or a day off if workers help the planet. They can give money to buy eco-friendly things like bikes or energy-saving lights. The company should say “thank you” to workers who care about the environment.

3. In what other ways could businesses involve their employees in environmental issues?
Companies can ask workers to join green teams or help with eco-projects. They should do clean-up days or tree planting events. Employees have to learn about the environment in meetings or workshops. Businesses would help more if they listen to their workers' green ideas.
